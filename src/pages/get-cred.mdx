# Welcome to the HPC Workshop

If you are attending the workshop as a guest, you will need a **temporary login credential** to access our HPC resources. 

Please follow the instructions below to retrieve your temporary username, password, and connection instructions.

:::warning Credential Expiration
Your temporary login credential will expire **7 days** after the workshop has finished. Please ensure you backup any data you wish to keep before your access is revoked.
:::
